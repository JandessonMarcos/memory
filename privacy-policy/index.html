Privacy Policy

This policy explains what Memory Lab collects, why, and the choices you have. It applies to memorylabdaily.com.

What we collect

Two things. First, if you use our "Memory Check" quiz and enter your email, we store that email along with your quiz answers so we can send your personalized result and occasional brain-health tips. Second, like most sites, we collect standard analytics: pages viewed, approximate location (country), device and browser type, and referring links.

How we use it

  • To send the result and follow-up emails you asked for. You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • To understand which articles are useful and improve the site.
  • To measure which sources bring readers, so we can keep publishing helpful content.

We do not sell your personal information.

Cookies and analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand traffic in aggregate. Analytics cookies help us count visits and see how pages perform. You can block cookies in your browser settings without losing access to the content.

Third parties

A few trusted services help us run the site: Cloudflare (hosting and security), Google Analytics (traffic measurement), and affiliate networks such as BuyGoods when you click a product link. Each has its own privacy policy governing the data it processes.

Your choices and rights

You can ask us to show, correct, or delete the email and quiz data we hold about you at any time. Depending on where you live (for example under GDPR or CCPA), you may have additional rights. To make a request, reach us through our contact page.

Changes

We may update this policy as the site evolves. The "Updated" date above reflects the latest revision.
