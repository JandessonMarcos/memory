About Memory Lab

Independent, plain-English guides on memory, focus and healthy aging — so you can tell what actually works from what's just marketing.

What we do

  • We translate cognitive-health research into clear, practical advice.
  • Every health claim is checked against published science.
  • Articles are reviewed by a medical professional for accuracy.
  • We disclose when a link is an affiliate link — our rankings are never for sale.

Our editorial standards

Memory Lab exists because the brain-health space is full of hype. Our job is to cut through it. We prioritize ingredient transparency, clinically-studied dosing and real user feedback over marketing claims. When we recommend a product, it's because it earned the spot on the criteria we publish openly in each review.

We are reader-supported. Some links on our site are affiliate links, meaning we may earn a commission if you buy through them, at no extra cost to you. This never influences our rankings or conclusions.
